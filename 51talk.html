What is 51Talk?

Asia's Leading Online English Platform

51Talk (NYSE: COE) is one of Asia's largest and most trusted online English education platforms. It connects thousands of English learners — primarily in China — with certified Filipino English teachers for one-on-one lessons.

As an official 51Talk partner, AID Virtual helps qualified Filipinos navigate the application and onboarding process, increasing your chances of acceptance and setting you up for long-term teaching success.

Our guidance is completely free — we succeed when our teachers succeed.

Am I Qualified?

51Talk Teacher Requirements

Check if you meet the basic qualifications. If you're unsure, reach out — we'll help you assess your eligibility.

✅ Basic Qualifications

✓
Bachelor's Degree Any field (Education is a plus but not required)
✓
Strong English Communication Skills Clear pronunciation and fluency in spoken and written English
✓
Stable Internet Connection Minimum 10 Mbps wired (LAN) connection recommended
✓
Quiet Teaching Environment A dedicated, noise-free space with good lighting for video classes
✓
Laptop or Desktop Computer With HD webcam and headset with microphone
